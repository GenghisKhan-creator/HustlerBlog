About Me

This is what I do.

Hey there! I'm Dimah, a programmer and web developer by day, and a gamer/knowledge enthusiast by… well, also by day (and sometimes night!). I graduated from Central University in 2023, and since then, I've been on a mission to conquer the tech world, one line of code at a time.

When I'm not building cool things on the web, I'm probably leveling up in some virtual world or diving headfirst into a research paper. I'm a firm believer in the power of continuous learning (and the importance of a good gaming session!), which is exactly why I created Hustlers University.

Think of it as my own little corner of the internet where I get to share all the interesting stuff I'm learning – my own research, articles, blog posts, the whole shebang. It's a place for anyone who loves to learn and explore new ideas. And the best part? You can join the hustle! If you've got some killer research, a thought-provoking article, or a blog post that's just waiting to be read, send it my way! We're always looking for fresh voices to feature on Hustlers University. So, whether you're a seasoned researcher or just starting out, we'd love to hear from you. Let's connect, share some knowledge, and make Hustlers University the go-to spot for curious minds!
